Our Story

THIS IS WHO WE ARE

Journey Office Builders has been serving Northern Virginia, Maryland and DC since 1989. Projects range from general office modification to total renovation including SCIF areas, server and multi-media rooms, cafeterias, restrooms, retail space and lobby renovations.

Our Leadership

Mike Miravi

President

Mike Miravi founded the business in 1989. Mr. Miravi graduated from Texas A&M with a Bachelor's of Science degree in Construction Management and has more than 30 years experience in the commercial construction industry.

Scott Johnson

Vice President

Scott Johnson joined the Company in 2007 after a fifteen year tenure as Senior Chief Engineer at CB Richard Ellis. His knowledge and experience of commercial construction prove to be a great asset to the Company. Mr. Johnson has a combined 33 years experience.
